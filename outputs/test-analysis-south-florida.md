## Top 5 Pain Points

### 1. Staffing & Retention
**Issue:** Tight labor market, hygienist/assistant shortages, high turnover costs, wage pressure from larger DSOs and corporate consolidation

### 2. Insurance Reimbursement Erosion
**Issue:** Declining fee schedules, slow claims processing, pre-authorization friction, payer mix challenges in a competitive market

### 3. Patient Acquisition & Competition
**Issue:** Heavy DSO/chain presence in South Florida, rising CAC due to marketing saturation, online reputation/review management demands

### 4. Technology & Compliance
**Issue:** Legacy EHR system costs/integration gaps, increasing regulatory burden (HIPAA, state licensing), cybersecurity/data breach risk exposure

### 5. Rising Operational Overhead
**Issue:** Rent/lease costs (especially Miami-Dade/Broward), utilities, supplies inflation, lab fees, compliance and continuing ed expenses squeezing margins

---

## Market Context

South Florida's market is heavily consolidated (high DSO density), competitive, and cost-intensive — independent practices struggle with scale disadvantages around staffing leverage, payer contracts, and tech infrastructure.
