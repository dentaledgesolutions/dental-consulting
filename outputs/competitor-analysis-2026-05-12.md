# Competitor Analysis — Southeast Florida Dental Market

**Date:** May 12, 2026
**Region:** Southeast Florida (Miami-Dade, Broward, Palm Beach Counties)

## Competitive Landscape Overview

Competitors fall into three distinct layers:

| Layer | Companies | Threat to DentalEdge |
|-------|-----------|----------------------|
| **SaaS / Practice Tech Platforms** | Weave, RevenueWell, Adit, Dental Intel, CareStack, MaxAssist, DentalRx | 🔴 High — established brands, dedicated sales teams |
| **AI-Powered Platforms** | Aron AI, Arini | 🟡 Medium — fast-growing, narrow focus |
| **Marketing Agencies / Consulting** | Wonderist Agency, Lasso MD, DDS Marketing, MedRank Interactive, WEO Media | 🟡 Medium — local presence but lack automation depth |
